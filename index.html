📚 What is RAG?

The Simple Analogy

  • Think of RAG as giving an AI a textbook to read before answering your question.
  • Instead of relying only on training data, you Retrieve relevant info first.
  • Then you Augment the AI prompt with that info.
  • Finally the AI Generates a grounded answer.

Why RAG Exists

  • Knowledge cutoff — LLMs don't know recent events.
  • Private data — LLMs don't know your company docs, emails, etc.
  • Hallucination — LLMs make up facts when uncertain.
  • RAG solves all three by providing real context from your documents.

📐 How Similarity Search Works
Query: "How to train a dog?"

Each stored chunk is compared with the query vector using cosine similarity. Score closer to 1.0 = more relevant.

"Puppies need training"
0.92 ✓ RETRIEVED
"Dogs are loyal pets"
0.78 ✓ RETRIEVED
"Python programming"
0.22 ✗ skipped

Cosine similarity = (A · B) / (|A| × |B|) → measures the angle between two vectors
